Research on Blech

The development of Blech is accompanied by research activities. This research is carried out in cooperation with different universities.

  • Synchronized Shared Memory and Procedural Abstraction: Towards a Formal Semantics of Blech. Friedrich Gretz, Franz-Josef Grosch, Michael Mendler and Stephan Scheele, 2020 Forum on Specification and Design Languages (FDL), Kiel, Germany. doi: 10.1109/FDL50818.2020.9232942

  • Blech, Imperative Synchronous Programming!. Friedrich Gretz and Franz-Josef Grosch, 2018 Forum on Specification & Design Languages (FDL), Garching, Germany. doi: 10.1109/FDL.2018.8524036 .
